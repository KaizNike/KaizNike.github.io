About Me:

I grew up in a little college town and started getting into trouble. Not drugs trouble, just weird trouble. All that time I was designing games, making art, having dreams. I kept a notebook of all the ideas I had and filled it up. I filled up notebooks a lot. I got my first computer around when I was seven, and oh man the games those days, such memories. I started programming around high school, my first projects being web pages and a asteroid game. Around that time I had decided to go to school for Computer Science, it sounded fun! My grades were good and I was prepared. The world however was a harsh place. Its been a long time since then, I got back into programming around 2019 after a job at Panera. I credit Exapunks for giving me the motive.
